Performance Opportunities

There are many big groups available to perform with. However, with big groups that tour they do not usually hold auditions often. Some of the big groups include:

The King's Singers

The King's Singers from Cambridge, England and is a British a cappella vocal ensemble founded in 1968.

The King's Singers

Chanticleer

Chanticleer is from San Francisco, CA and is a full-time male classical vocal ensemble in the United States. Currently, Chanticleer comprises twelve men, including two basses, one baritone, three tenors, and six countertenors (three altos and three sopranos).

Chanticleer

Messiah

Another opportunity is the production of the "Messiah" by George Frideric Handel.
