Site Plan

Project Purpose

The purpose of this web site is to inform an audience, from a musical stand point, exact what is a countertenor. It will provide a little biography of how I realized I was one and will provide other famous countertenors of this era. Another page will describe the origins of countertenors and why it was so popular a voice type.

Wireframe Sketches

Usability Concepts

  • I will have my font size at minimum 16pt. Other fonts will be bigger on the basis of importance.
  • My image ALT tags will be very simple and understandable if the image does not show up.
  • My links will be consistent in the manner of their placement on each page and will be understandable.
  • My headings per section will be a very simple description of the information that they would be looking for.
  • My copy will be short, simple, and to the point. It will be in a manner that they will find the information they are looking for and be able to take away that content.
