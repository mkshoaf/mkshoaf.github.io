Purpose Statement

The purpose of this site is to provide the viewer with accurate weather for the day, in the city they are living.

Audience Description

Average age range is in the mid to late 30s but between 25 and 50. Middle Class income range. Casual people that are looking for a good day for the beach. They usually access the site by their phone or tablet.
